# Features

A comprehensive overview of the application's capabilities, organized by module.

## üè† Dashboard
The central hub for financial health, providing a high-level overview of your net worth and spending habits.
- **Financial Health Score**: An aggregate metric indicating your overall financial stability.
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Proactive, intelligent suggestions and observations about your spending patterns.
- **Net Worth Overview**: Real-time calculation of assets minus liabilities.
- **Quick Actions**: Fast access to common tasks like uploading receipts or adding transactions.

## üåç Pockets (Asset & Liability Tracking)
A specialized module for managing physical assets and liabilities, categorized into four key areas.

### ‚úàÔ∏è Travel
- **Interactive World Map**: Visualizes the countries you have visited based on transaction data.
- **Spending Geography**: Automatically distinguishes between "Domestic" and "Abroad" spending.
- **Trip Analytics**: detailed breakdown of travel-related expenses by country.

### üöó Garage
- **Fleet Management**: Track multiple vehicles with detailed metadata (Brand, Model, Year, VIN, License Plate).
- **Total Cost of Ownership**: Granular tracking of all vehicle interactions:
  - **Fuel**: Track tank fills, fuel type, and efficiency.
  - **Maintenance**: Log repairs and service costs.
  - **Insurance**: Track premiums and renewal dates.
  - **Parking & Certificates**: Manage tolls, parking fees, and registration costs.
- **Loan Tracking**: specialized fields for vehicle financing, including interest rates, loan terms, and remaining balance.

### üè† Property
- **Real Estate Portfolio**: Manage both **Owned** and **Rented** properties.
- **Mortgage Management**: Deep tracking of mortgage details:
  - Original Loan Amount
  - Interest Rates & Terms
  - Years Paid vs. Remaining
- **Equity Visualization**: Automatically calculates and visualizes your home equity based on estimated value vs. remaining mortgage.

### üì¶ Other Assets
- **Custom Collections**: Flexible system to track any other valuable items (e.g., Art, Electronics, Collectibles).
- **Valuation Tracking**: Keep a history of estimated values for miscellaneous assets.

## ü•¶ Fridge (Grocery Intelligence)
A dedicated engine for analyzing grocery spending at the item level.
- **Smart Receipt Scanning**: Upload receipt images for AI-powered parsing (OCR). Automatically extracts merchant, date, and individual line items.
- **Interactive Review System**: A human-in-the-loop interface to verify and correct AI-parsed receipts before they hit your ledger.
- **Item-Level Categorization**: Classify individual grocery items (e.g., "Bananas" -> "Fruit", "Chips" -> "Snacks").
- **Nutrition & Spending Trends**: Analyze spending habits based on nutritional value (e.g., "Nutritious" vs. "Snacks") and store preferences.
- **Spending Limits**: Set specific budget caps for grocery categories to control costs.

## üí∞ Savings & Debt
- **Accumulation Analysis**: Visual charts showing the growth of your savings strategies over time.
- **Mortgage Calculator**: Built-in financial tool to plan for property purchases, estimating payments and interest.
- **Debt Overview**: A consolidated view of all liabilities to help formulate payoff strategies.

## üìö Data Library
The backend engine for data management, giving you full control over your financial data.
- **Unified Transaction Ledger**: A powerful, searchable, and filterable view of every transaction across all accounts.
- **Statement Import Pipeline**: Robust CSV importer for bank statements, supporting drag-and-drop and AI interpretation of messy bank descriptions.
- **Smart Categorization**:
  - **Needs vs. Wants**: Tag categories to analyze discretionary vs. essential spending.
  - **Custom Hierarchies**: Create and manage custom category trees.
- **Receipt Management**: Manage receipt types and specific receipt categories independent of general bank transaction categories.
- **Reports**: Generate and export data summaries for external use.
